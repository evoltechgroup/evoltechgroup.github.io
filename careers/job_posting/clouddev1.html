Roles and Responsibilities
  • Build, migrate and deploy containerized applications to Kubernetes/OpenShift, the leading Container Orchestration (CO) platform..
  • Integrate a diverse range of solutions for data persistence, security, scalability, monitoring and logging, SIEM, APIs and messaging flows by multiple cloud providers (AWS, Azure as well as on-prem).
  • Develop testing strategies and manage devops pipelines for many of the biggest and most complex ISV and Partner systems.
  • Help to install and administer OpenShift on industry-leading cloud platforms.
  • Demonstrate your passion for learning, experimenting, and implementing innovating frontend, middleware and backend technologies.
  • Be directly exposed to and interact with diverse architectures and production systems by ISVs and Partners and work with their architects and engineers.
  • Work in many different technology areas and have the opportunity to build skills and experience in areas that really excite you.
  • Work on multiple cloud providers like AWS, Azure, GCP.
  • Have access to all the technical and professional training courses and certifications to become an expert in your field.
  • Be able to work on multiple strategic areas in Cloud & AI.
  • Interface and collaborate with Client / Partner opportunities.
  • Have opportunities to understand and learn new technologies from SMEs and leading ISVs and partners.
Required Technical and Professional Expertise
  • Minimum 10 years of relevant experience.
  • Experience working with one of the public cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Experience working with containers and Kubernetes or OpenShift.
  • Experience working with bash or powerscript for scripting.
  • Experience working with Git-based SCM.
  • Experience working with Agile development methodologies across multi-disciplinary teams.
  • Experience of working with build pipelines.
  • Understanding or experience of working with a database management system.
  • Understanding or experience of automating provisioning & configuration management of infrastructure with tools like Terraform, Ansible, Puppet, Chef.
  • Understanding or experience with modernization and/or migration of applications to one or more of the public cloud providers (AWS, Azure, GCP.).
  • Understanding infrastructure, networking and security concepts.
  • Software Development experience in one or more of Java, C#, Python, Go, JavaScript/Node.JS.
  • Experience with Service Mesh, Helm.
  • Experience with SQL, NoSQL, in-memory and/or caching.
  • Experience with Jenkins, Tekton, GitOps, AWS DevOps, Azure DevOps (ADO) or equivalent.
  • Experience with networking and security for containers, Kubernetes or OpenShift.
Preferred Technical and Professional Expertise
  • Experience with operator-sdk, Go or Ansible-based Operators.
  • Experience with REST APIs and event-driven messaging like Kafka, Mosquitto, HiveMQ, MQ or other.
  • Experience with Kubernetes observability stack, like ELK, Instana, Dynatrace, or DataDog.
  • Experience with networking configurations like DNS, DHCP, TCP/IP, routing, switching, HTTP.
  • Experience working with system administration tasks.
  • Active listening, negotiation, problem-solving.
  • Team building
Education
  • B.Tech/B.E. in Any Specialization (Computer Science major is preferred)
